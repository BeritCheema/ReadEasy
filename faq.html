FAQ for AI-Powered Accessibility Add-on

Is the add-on compatible with all Google Docs?

Yes, the add-on works with all Google Docs. It seamlessly integrates with the Google Docs interface, allowing you to apply dyslexia-friendly settings to any document.

Is there a cost associated with using this add-on?

The add-on is completely free to use and we are a non-profit committed to helping people with dyslexia and other visual impairments.

How does the add-on benefit users with dyslexia?

The add-on provides custom settings like dyslexia-friendly fonts, adjusts text size and spacing, and color overlays to reduce visual stress and improve readability for users with dyslexia. We also harnesses the power of AI to make texts simpler and easier to read.

Where can I find support or report issues with the add-on?

If you need assistance or wish to report an issue, please contact our support team through our website and send us an email. We are committed to providing prompt and helpful support.
